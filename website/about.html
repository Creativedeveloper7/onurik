Onurik Systems

Principal Architect & Design Director

With over a decade shaping brands and engineering systems, I’ve built a way of working that understands both structure and story—how things look, how they function, and how they hold over time.

My focus is rooted in the Sub-Saharan market, particularly East and Central Africa, where I’ve worked closely with businesses building for real impact. This is where I’ve learned what it means to design with context. Not borrowed ideas, but systems and brands that feel ours—built to hold, sustain, and grow within the environments they serve.

At Onurik, we approach every project as both builders and learners. We take on complex challenges, stay through the uncertainty, and refine until the system works the way it should. No shortcuts. No surface-level solutions. Just work that stands.

We design, we engineer, and we see it through—because the goal isn’t just to launch, but to last.

We are Africans, and Africa is our business.
